🧩 What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the authorised practice of bypassing system security to identify potential data breaches and threats in a network. It is essential for securing systems, building cyber resilience, and protecting sensitive data.

💡 Why is it Important?

As cyberattacks grow more sophisticated, ethical hacking ensures proactive defence by finding vulnerabilities before malicious hackers do. It supports organisations in securing networks, ensuring compliance, and maintaining trust.
